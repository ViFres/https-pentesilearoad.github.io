The Band

About Us

Pentesilea Road was born as a heavy prog band. The name references Pentesilea — the diluted city described in Italo Calvino's Invisible Cities — a metaphor for the sense of displacement in the post-modern era.

The debut single "Shades of the Night" was released on January 29th, 2021. The eponymous debut album followed on February 26th, 2021, featuring Ray Alder, Mark Zonder, Michele Guaitoli and Lorenzo Vincenzo Nocerino as special guests.

In 2023 the line-up consolidated around Michele Guaitoli (Visions of Atlantis, Temperance) on vocals and Giovanni Montesano (Cirque du Soleil) on bass. Three years of composing and recording yielded the double album Sonnets from the Drowsiness, released May 23rd, 2025.
