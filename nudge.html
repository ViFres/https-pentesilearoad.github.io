The People

Bios

Vito F. Mainolfi

Founder · Musician · Composer

A visionary musician and multi-instrumentalist best known as the founder of Pentesilea Road. With a passion for progressive rock, alternative soundscapes, and experimental music, Vito has crafted a distinct artistic identity blending technical mastery with raw emotion. Originally launching The Nudge Unit as a solo, boundary-pushing project, he expanded the sound alongside talented collaborators — and for the first time steps into vocals, adding a new expressive dimension to his artistry.

Hans Duynhoven

Keyboards · Sound Arrangements

Dutch keyboardist who began his career at 17 in pop groups before becoming a professional musician and educator. After studying Hammond Organ, Jazz Piano, and Keyboards at the Leeuwarden Conservatory, he taught at multiple music schools and conservatories. For the last twenty years of his career he was affiliated with the Academy of Pop Culture in Leeuwarden, supervising projects and teaching Keys and Music Theory. He currently works as a freelancer contributing to studio projects.

Mark Zonder

Drums

Legendary drummer known for his work with Fates Warning and Warlord. A guest on the debut Pentesilea Road album, Mark returns here to play drums on five of the eight tracks, bringing his signature precision and power to The Nudge Unit's sound.

Jason Dykes

Guest Keyboard Solos

Featured on American Wake and Feathers, Jason contributes expressive keyboard solos that add a distinctive colour to the album's most atmospheric moments.
